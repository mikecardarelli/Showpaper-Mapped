About Showpaper

Showpaper was a free bi-weekly publication, distributed in 190+ print issues from 2007 to 2014, that connected its readers to every all-ages and under-$25 concert across NYC and the tri-state area. Each issue paired show listings with full-color artwork from contemporary visual artists, making Showpaper both a practical resource and a collectible piece of cultural history.
